## Key Concepts Learned

Multiplying factors:

Increase x% → multiply by (1 + x/100)
Decrease x% → multiply by (1 − x/100)

Examples:

* Increase 40% → ×1.4
* Discount 50% → ×0.5
* Discount 25% → ×0.75

Combined change example:
40% markup followed by 50% discount

= 1.4 × 0.5
= 0.7

Meaning the final price becomes **70% of the previous price**.
